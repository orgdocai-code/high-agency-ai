Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do I need to be technical?+
Not at all. We explain everything in plain English and focus on business outcomes. If you're non-technical, you're exactly who we designed these services for.
What can AI actually do for my business?+
Automate customer responses, generate content, analyze data, process documents, schedule meetings, qualify leads, and much more. We'll identify what's realistic for YOUR business.
How quickly will I see results?+
Most businesses see value within 2-4 weeks. Simple AI implementations can show results in days. We'll give you a realistic timeline during the discovery phase.
Do you build custom AI models?+
No. We focus on practical implementations using existing platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, OpenClaw, etc.). This keeps costs down and delivers faster results.
What's the difference between the service packages?+
AI Quick Start is for businesses wanting to explore AI. AI Implementation is for those ready to deploy. AI Partner is for ongoing support. Salesforce Implementation is for CRM needs. IT Solutions covers broader technical consulting.
What if AI isn't right for my business?+
We'll tell you. We're not here to sell you something you don't need. The AI Quick Start gives you an honest assessment of whether AI makes sense for your business.
